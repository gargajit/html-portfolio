The Best Movies According to Ajit

My top 3 novies of all-time.

Before Series

A triology which is not just a love story of a young American meeting a French Girl on a train.
It's a life journey that teaches some valuable lessons.

Interstellar

Christopher Nolan's masterpiece.
It excites the viewers about the logics and facts of the space-time and dimensions. Everything is so beautiful, the story, the script, the acting and the soundtrack by Hans Zimmer.

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

An Indian movie with some amazing adventures of friends and songs. Life is all about friends becoming family.
